Why Software Engineering?

Being the first employee at a VC-backed startup exposed me to the challenges of transforming great ideas into a market-ready product. This experience demanded resourcefulness and gave me a deep understanding of what it takes to build a successful venture from the ground up.

In my role as Head of Operations, I worked with everyone from junior engineers to CEOs around the world. These interactions gave me a broad perspective on the tech landscape and sharpened my ability to drive growth and efficiency in fast-paced environments.

Over time, I became increasingly aware of coding's potential to create scalable solutions that can impact millions. My interest in addressing global challenges, inspired by longtermism, led me to pursue software engineering as a way to make a meaningful contribution and build a career aligned with my values.
